Hello, world
July 4, 2026

I take notes on most papers I read. This time I wanted to write them down in public, for sharing my work, personal visibility, and accountability. Most of what I write here is exactly that: notes, kept in the open. The excerpt at the top of each post is where my own thoughts and conclusions go.

There are also more reasons to share your thoughts now than before. Getting your blog in front of people should keep getting easier, because AI can search across many blogs, read them, and bring the relevant ones to you.

Lastly, the writing is technical. I assume you are somewhat familiar with the field, not just getting into it. Where a post leaves gaps, I hope you can play Q&A with the AI bot to fill them in.

Sometimes a post may be unclear, inaccurate, imprecise, or something you disagree with. In those cases, the best thing you can do is tell me.
